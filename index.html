I’m Jonathan Lee Martin, a globetrotting digital nomad and fine art landscape photographer from Atlanta, GA. By day I work as a developer & international trainer, but at twilight I scale mountains to discover unique landscapes.

Five years ago I didn’t plan to globetrot, but the tourist-clogged cities drove me to the mountains where, through the medium of photography, I stoke my wanderlust and blog about these escapades to awaken it in others.

I love meeting people from all over the world! Say hello to hi@yellowscale.com or connect on social media.
